# **RayhanADev's Ask Me Anything**

---

## How to Ask?

Just [submit](https://github.com/RayhanADev/ama/issues) an issue with the title as your question and anything extra in the description!

---

## What Can I Ask?
1. Nothing awkwardly personal (my address, phone number, etc)
2. Coding or Designing Tips
3. My Project's Inspiration/Development Process
4. Coding History
5. Future Plans
6. or something awesomely unique!

---
